# Case Facts: Bobov vs. УК «Новоуральская» (12.4M RUB)

## Chronology of Events
1.  **2003-2012**: Oleg Bobov occupies the basement of Novouralsk Nautical Club for vessel construction and storage.
2.  **22.01.2013**: УК «Новоуральская» (Management Company) breaks the locks and enters the premises under the guise of "utility maintenance" and "clearing trash."
3.  **23.01.2013**: First formal "Refusal to Initiate Criminal Case" (Отказ в возбуждении УД) issued by the police.
4.  **2013-2023**: Continuous cycle of filing complaints, temporary cancellations of the refusal, and new refusals by the police.
5.  **25.03.2026**: Current уточненная жалоба (refined complaint) filed to the Prosecutor naming specific officers.
6.  **01.04.2026**: Final Notary Ultimatum (Ультиматум Ислентьевой И.В.) delivered, establishing a "point of no return" for asset recovery.

## Asset Inventory (Valuation 12,401,207 RUB)
- **Trimarans (F-31)**: 4 hulls in various stages of construction. Custom-built, high-value nautical assets.
- **Machinery**: Industrial woodworking and metalworking machines. Professionally maintained equipment.
- **Intellectual Property**: Blueprints, technical documentation for vessel design (1980-2012).
- **Consumables**: High-grade composites, rigging equipment, boat building materials.

## Opponents and Negligent Officers
- **УК «Новоуральская» (Director)**: Ислентьева И.В. Target of the Notary Ultimatum.
- **Police Investigation (Negligence)**: Меньшиков Г.Р., Мовланов Т.Р. Named for Art. 293 UK РФ (Халатность).

## 2024–2025 Legal Reclassification (Theft vs Self-rule)
- **Art. 158 (Theft)**: Requires "greedy intent" (корыстный умысел). The **lack of inventory (опись)** during the 2013 seizure is the primary evidence that the intention was not "cleaning trash" but permanent dispossession/theft.
- **Art. 330 (Self-rule)**: Traditionally applied to УК actions (expired). Reclassification to Art. 158 Part 4 (10-year statute of limitations) is now reinforced by court trends where "silence" in documentation equals "intent to steal."
- **FZ-68**: 13 years of "reasonable time" violation enables claims against the state treasury.
