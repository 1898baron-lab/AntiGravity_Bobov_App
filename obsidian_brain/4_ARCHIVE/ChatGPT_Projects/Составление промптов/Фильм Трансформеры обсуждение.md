He is wearing simple black prescription eyeglasses.

The glasses are suddenly pulled off his face and stop in mid-air in front of him.

A complex multi-stage mechanical transformation begins.
The temples detach, extend, split into multiple segments, and rotate around the frame.
The front frame separates into panels; hinges unfold, rails extend, metallic parts slide and reconfigure.
The lenses rotate forward, darken, expand, and reshape into rigid armored segments.

The glasses fully transform into a futuristic combat mask inspired by Sub-Zero style: angular, symmetrical, cold metallic design, covering the mouth and lower face.

The mask sits perfectly on the face, aligned and sealed.

Ultra-realistic cinematic VFX, Transformers-inspired mechanical logic, high part count, realistic physics.
Dramatic lighting, shallow depth of field, no magic, no instant morph, no fantasy glow.
